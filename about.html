About

Who We Are

Frangain™ (FRANG) is a cultural, symbolic token on the BNB Smart Chain. It’s a digital tribute to the Lebanese “Frang”—a small coin that became part of everyday language, memory, and folklore. Frangain™ exists to preserve that story on-chain with dignity and clarity.

Our Mission

  • Preserve cultural memory through modern, open blockchain tools.
  • Support small grants for learning, creativity, and digital archiving.
  • Share the story widely—without hype, with heart.

What Frangain™ Is / Isn’t

Frangain™ is: cultural, educational, community-first.

Frangain™ isn’t: financial advice, a promise of profit, or a trading scheme.
