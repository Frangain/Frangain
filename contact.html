Frangain is a cultural token project dedicated to preserving the historical memory of the Lebanese "Frang" coin in a digital form. The project focuses on heritage, symbolism, and educational awareness in the crypto space.

Token Information

  • Symbol: FRANG
  • Network: BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)

  • Contract Address:
