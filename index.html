What is Frangain™?

A symbolic BEP-20 token that revives a cultural memory of Lebanon.

Frangain™ commemorates the historic Lebanese "Frang™" — a coin that once echoed through souks and homes. This project isn’t about speculation, but about honoring a shared heritage. It's a symbol, a digital relic, and a spark for community-led learning and creativity.
