What is the Circle of Trust?

The Circle of Trust is the planned community layer of the FRANGAIN Ecosystem.

The Circle of Trust will help connect real supporters around the project mission: cultural memory, responsible participation, and long-term community building. It is informational at this stage and does not include referral bonuses or any active reward logic.

FAQ

What is Memory Mining?

Memory Mining is the planned mechanism for users to mine FRANG and build their FRANG Reserve. It is not active on this portal page.

Is FRANGAIN free?

Creating an ecosystem account is designed to be simple and accessible. Token purchases remain separate from account registration.

Do I need crypto experience?

No. The ecosystem is being built to introduce concepts clearly and gradually.

What is the Circle of Trust?

It is the planned community layer for trusted participation and future social features.

How do I install FRANGAIN?

Use the Install FRANGAIN action when your browser supports native installation. On iPhone, use Share and Add to Home Screen.
